  • Measured worn freeform surface of orthopaedic prostheses by utilizing instruments like CMM, CT-Scanner and Profilometer.

  • Tested the fitting of reconstructed surface on the measured surface to ensure the deviation is less than 5 microns.
  • Characterized linear wear as low as 0.005 mm and volumetric wear as low as 0.4 mm3.
